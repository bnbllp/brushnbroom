Our Mission

Community Care Program

We believe everyone deserves a clean, safe place to live. That's why we offer free and discounted cleanings for elderly individuals, disabled people, veterans, and those facing hardship. Giving back isn't something we plan to do someday — it's something we're already doing.

Who We Help

Elderly Individuals

We provide free or deeply discounted cleanings for seniors on fixed incomes who may struggle with physical maintenance of their homes.

Disabled Individuals

For those with disabilities that make cleaning difficult or impossible, we step in to help keep their homes safe and livable.

Veterans

We honor those who served by offering free cleanings to veterans facing housing instability or eviction due to unclean conditions.

Those Facing Hardship

If you're experiencing financial hardship, homelessness, or facing eviction, we're here to help you maintain a clean, dignified home.

How It Works

Our process is simple, confidential, and designed with dignity in mind. There's no judgment, no complicated paperwork, just genuine help.

1

Reach Out

Call us at 206-571-5568 or email seabnbllp@brushnbroom.com. Tell us about your situation — we listen without judgment.

2

We Assess

We'll discuss your needs and determine the best way we can help, whether that's a free cleaning or a discounted service.

3

We Schedule

We'll find a time that works for you and show up ready to work. Our team treats your home with the same respect we'd give our own.

4

We Deliver

We complete the cleaning with care and attention to detail, leaving your home fresh, clean, and ready for living.

Our Commitment

Confidentiality: Your situation is private. We don't share information or make judgments.

Dignity: You deserve respect. We treat every person with the same care and professionalism we'd show anyone else.

Quality: Free doesn't mean less. We bring the same attention to detail and care to community cleanings as we do to all our work.
