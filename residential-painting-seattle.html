Our Process

Good painting starts before the first coat.

We keep the process simple and honest. After learning about your project, we review the surfaces, talk through prep needs, protect the surrounding space, paint carefully, and leave the area tidy when the work is complete.

1

Clear project review

We discuss rooms, surfaces, colors, timing, access, photos, and any repairs or touch-ups that may affect the scope.

2

Protection and prep

We protect floors, furniture, fixtures, landscaping, and unpainted surfaces, then prep the areas so paint has a cleaner foundation.

3

Careful painting

We focus on coverage, clean edges, trim details, doors, corners, and the small areas that are easy to notice when a project is rushed.

4

Tidy finish

We remove masking, clean up the work area, and review the finished result so your home feels refreshed instead of disrupted.
