Our Story

Built on grit, second chances, and giving back.

Hi, we're Dustin and Brittany, and we're the husband-and-wife team behind Brush & Broom LLP. Our journey has been anything but ordinary, taking us from Michigan to Alabama, and finally, to building a new life here in Seattle. We believe in honest work, clear communication, and treating every home like our own.

Who We Are

From humble beginnings to a fresh start in Seattle.

Dustin honed his construction roots back in his home state of Michigan, starting as a finish carpenter specializing in bathroom and kitchen remodels. He later joined the painters union in Alabama, gaining precision and expertise, all while earning a business degree from the University of Alabama and obtaining his universal EPA certification for AC systems.

Life took an unexpected turn when Brittany and I moved to Seattle with our three kids. We faced unforeseen hardships, including a period of homelessness. But we're not ones to back down from a challenge. We toughed it out in a shelter, channeling every ounce of grit into building a fresh start for our family and our business.

On January 30, 2025, we proudly relaunched Brush & Broom LLP. We saw a gap in the Seattle home services market – people were tired of being treated like a number by large franchises. They wanted someone they could trust in their home, someone who actually cared about the finished result and showed up when they said they would. That's exactly what we set out to be.

Dustin: The "Brush"

Painting & Operations

With union-honed precision, I lead the "brush" side of our business. I ensure every painting job, whether interior or exterior, meets our high standards for prep and finish. I take pride in clean lines and honest work, using trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams to make your home feel new again.

Brittany: The "Broom"

Cleaning & Management

As the other half of Brush & Broom LLP, Brittany owns the "broom." She personally oversees our cleaning services, ensuring every detail matters and your home feels truly refreshed. From eco-friendly deep cleans to move-ins, Brittany builds trusted client bonds and makes sure you're not just a ticket number – you're our neighbor.

Our Values

What matters most to us

Real Communication

No runaround. We give clear quotes and keep you updated throughout the process. If something changes, you will hear it from us first.

Respect for Your Home

We treat your space like our own, ensuring it is protected and left better than we found it. That is a promise, not a tagline.

Work Done Right

We do not cut corners. Whether it is a deep clean or a full repaint, we focus on quality because your home deserves it.

Giving Back

More than a business — a mission.

We believe everyone deserves a clean, safe place to live. That is why giving back is not something we plan to do someday — it is something we are already doing.

Helping People Keep Their Homes

We have cleaned homes for free for disabled individuals facing eviction due to living conditions. When someone is at risk of losing their home over something we can fix, we show up — no invoice, no strings attached.

Supporting Homeless Families

Our long-term vision is to help homeless communities, especially families with children and the most vulnerable. We are building toward that goal with every job we take, because the work we do today funds the difference we want to make tomorrow.
