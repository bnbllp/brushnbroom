Our Story

Built on grit, second chances, and giving back.

Hi, we're Dustin and Brittany, and we're the husband-and-wife team behind Brush & Broom LLP. Our journey has been anything but ordinary, leading us through real hardship before we planted roots here in Seattle. We believe in honest work, clear communication, and treating every home like our own.

Who We Are

From humble beginnings to a fresh start in Seattle.

I honed my construction roots starting as a finish carpenter specializing in bathroom and kitchen remodels. I later joined the painters union, gaining precision and expertise in the trades, all while earning a business degree and obtaining my universal EPA certification for AC systems.

Life took an unexpected turn when my wife Brittany and I moved to Seattle with our three kids. We faced unforeseen hardships, which brought a period of homelessness. But we're not ones to back down from a challenge or chaos. We toughed it out in a tent for months before getting into a shelter, channeling every ounce of grit into building a fresh start for our family and for Brush and Broom.

On January 30, 2025, we proudly launched Brush & Broom LLP. We saw a need for genuine and honest service businesses in the Seattle home services market. People are tired of being treated like a number by large franchises. They wanted someone they can trust in their home, someone who actually cares about the finished result and shows up when they say they will. Aside from many other things, that's exactly what we set out to do.

Dustin: The "Brush"

Operations & Projects

With union-honed precision and a background in home improvement trades, I lead the "brush" side of our business by handling operations, estimates, and quality control. My focus is making sure every cleaning project is organized, safe, and done right.

Brittany: The "Broom"

Cleaning & Management

As the other half of Brush & Broom LLP, Brittany owns the "broom." She personally oversees our cleaning services, ensuring every detail matters and your home feels truly refreshed. From deep cleans to move-ins and move-outs, Brittany builds trusted client bonds and makes sure you're not just a ticket number – you're our neighbor.

Our Values

What matters most to us

Real Communication

No runaround. We give clear quotes and keep you updated throughout the process. If something changes, you will hear it from us first.

Respect for Your Home

We treat your space like our own, ensuring it is protected and left better than we found it. That is a promise, not a tagline.

Work Done Right

We do not cut corners. Whether it is a deep clean or a full home reset, we focus on quality because your home deserves it.

Giving Back

More than a business — a mission.

We believe everyone deserves a clean, safe place to live. That is why giving back is not something we plan to do someday — it is something we are already doing.

Helping People Keep Their Homes

We have cleaned homes for free for disabled individuals facing eviction due to living conditions. When someone is at risk of losing their home over something we can fix, we show up — no invoice, no strings attached.

Supporting Homeless Families

Our long-term vision is to help homeless communities, especially families with children and the most vulnerable. We are building toward that goal with every job we take, because the work we do today funds the difference we want to make tomorrow.
