Our Process

How we handle your exterior project

1. Free On-Site Quote

We visit your home, inspect the exterior, and provide a clear, detailed estimate. No pressure, no surprises — just honest pricing.

2. Surface Preparation

We pressure wash, scrape loose paint, sand rough areas, fill cracks, and apply primer. This is the most critical step for a lasting finish.

3. Professional Application

Two coats of premium paint applied with care. We protect your landscaping, windows, and fixtures throughout the process.

4. Final Walkthrough

We walk through the completed project with you to ensure everything meets your expectations. We clean up completely before we leave.
